About Us

Welcome to Kairali Medicals and Surgicals! Since 2002, we have been your trusted pharmacy, providing top-quality medicines and surgical supplies. Located conveniently opposite Deluxe Payward, Trivandrum Medical College, we pride ourselves on offering personalized care, genuine products, and swift service. Our goal is to make healthcare accessible by offering medicine delivery to your doorstep. Order online with us for a hassle-free experience.

Terms and Conditions

  • A valid prescription must be provided to place an order.
  • Orders can only be placed between 9:00 AM and 8:00 PM IST.
  • Orders will be delivered within 2 to 3 business days.
  • Delivery is facilitated via India Post's Speed Post service.
  • Only prepaid orders are accepted. Payments can be made via Google Pay.
  • Medicines once delivered cannot be returned or exchanged.
  • By placing an order, the customer agrees to all terms and conditions. T&C apply.*
